Who we are?

Ikabit is the future of communications for Dental Pracices. Our solution enables practices to send and receive messages hands-free without interrupting the task at hand. Leaving you to do what you do best.

Overview

Ikabit piggybacks on existing wireless technology to provide a headset-based system that doesn't require users to carry an additional device--not even a cellphone. Our system is controlled via voice recognition so users can send a voice message or chat live with a specific group or individual--all without interrupting the task at hand. It connects users only to the person or people that need to hear the message. Our proprietary innovations also cost less than half of other workplace communication options available in the market today.

How It Works

Our Pod

Our product utilizes our "Pod" and your off the shelf bluetooth devices (i.e. bluetooth headsets or watches). Our Pod currently utilizes a raspberry pi 3 model B single-board computer with wireless LAN and bluetooth connectivity. The pod utilizes voice recognition technology and communicates with a server to send messages to and from specific users or groups. Any bluetooth device used for communication that is bluetooth 4.0 or higher is supported.

Bluetooth Devices

Currently any device that is bluetooth 4.0 or higher, or Wifi enabled is supported. You will be able to receive messages from a Bluetooth or Wifi enabled headset, a text message on your phone, a message on your smart watch or even an email. Every individual user can set their own device preference as well. The pod communicates with the server to find where you are at, what devices you have active and sends the message to you accordingly.

The Software

The software and all of your settings/preferences can be adjusted inside of our user-friendly dashboard. You can add/modify/remove user and group data, create and send messages, create templated messages that are used frequently, change your wifi settings, display pod and headset information, update your pods and servers, and also submit support requests. Our dashboard gives you full control on how you want to run your network.
